Pinakothek der Moderne

Difficult to say which museum is best in Munich, but this one gives a nice overview.

Fancy industrial design? Check. Sculptures where you don't know top from bottom? Check. Great Austrian painters? Check. This and many more art installations can be found in Pinakothek der Moderne.

My favourite part: The Collection of applied modernist art was founded in 1925. With around 70.000 objects of industrial design, graphic design and the arts and crafts the "Neue Sammlung" is today one of the world's leading museums of 20th century applied art, and indeed the largest of industrial design. Parts of the expanded collection are exhibited in the basement of the Pinakothek der Moderne. Among others objects about motor vehicle design, computer culture, design of artistic jewelry and furnitures (e.g. the collection of chairs of Michael Thonet) are exhibited.
